About FloodGuard India

Empowering communities with data-driven disaster preparedness and real-time flood intelligence.

Every life lost to floods is a failure of information, not nature. We exist to bridge that gap — turning raw environmental data into actionable community intelligence.
— FloodGuard India Vision

Why Flood Awareness?

India faces devastating floods annually, affecting over 40 million people on average. With climate change intensifying monsoon patterns, real-time flood intelligence is no longer optional — it's a necessity. Our platform bridges the gap between raw environmental data and actionable community insights, helping save lives and reduce economic losses that exceed ₹50,000 crore annually.

How Data Helps

By aggregating weather data, river gauge measurements, satellite imagery, and historical flood records, we create comprehensive flood risk assessments. Machine learning models analyze patterns across decades of data to improve prediction accuracy. Early warning systems powered by this data have been shown to reduce flood casualties by up to 50% when communities are prepared.

Data Sources

FloodGuard India integrates data from multiple authoritative sources:

  • Open-Meteo API — Real-time weather forecasts
  • India Meteorological Department (IMD)
  • Central Water Commission (CWC)
  • National Remote Sensing Centre (NRSC)
  • OpenStreetMap — Geographic mapping data
  • India Flood Inventory (IFI) — Historical records
