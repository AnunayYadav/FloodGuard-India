Wildlife Impact (Kaziranga)

70-90% Park Area Affected Yearly

Every monsoon, the Brahmaputra floods Kaziranga National Park. In extreme years, 200+ animals (Rhinos, Elephants, Deer) perish.

  • Tigers move to higher villages (Conflict)
  • Elephants migrate long distances
  • High deaths during highway crossings
